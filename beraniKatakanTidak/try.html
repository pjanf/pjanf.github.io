TextTweener Example

This is a very long sample text with a lot of words and some "1, 2, 3" numbers - to test the function of TextTweener! The second text will then be created smoothly with the letters from the first text. Now with special "characters" like 1 2 3 : ? or _ ! And after that there is a third text to watch the transition again - with some special characters: ? 5 2 9 1 _ ! If you like, you can change the duration of one text by using the option duration.
